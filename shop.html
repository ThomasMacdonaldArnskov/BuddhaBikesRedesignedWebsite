THE BICYCLE WORKSHOP WHERE PASSION,
PROFESSIONALISM
AND CREATIVITY
IS A LIFESTYLE


At Buddha Bikes we always deliver professional quality work. All our mechanics have completed the education as Bicycle Mechanics approved by the Ministry of Education. We at Buddha Bikes have two things in common: The passion for cycling and eternally good mood. We try to only use recycled bicycle parts, to appreciate our vision of sustainable reuse of all material goods, but we do not compromise with the quality of the bicycle parts if a new one is needed. That why you always can rely one the Buddha Bike family.

We can repair almost any type of bike so when you need it, just come by with yours. That includes Christiania and other cargo bikes, longjohns, racing bikes, mountain bikes, childrens bikes etc.

The price list just represents some of the more common reparations - so don't worry if your problem is not on there. We are proud to take on almost any challenge so if other bike workshops tell you "no", try to talk to us instead.
