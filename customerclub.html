THE BUDDHA BIKES
CUSTOMER CLUB


As a member of our customer club you get a 20% discount on all future reparations on bikes bought from Buddha Bikes. It is free to become a member and very easy to become one.


Learn about the customer club



If this sound enticing why not join our Buddha Bikes family and sign up for the customer club? If you donate your old bike you will furthermore get either a new lock, some nice lights or even a discount on your next purchase - as a way of us saying thank you.
