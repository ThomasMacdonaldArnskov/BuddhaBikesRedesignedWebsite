BUY RECYCLED BIKES, WHICH
ARE AS GOOD AS NEW


All bikes made by Buddha Bikes are reincernated second hand bikes. The old metal end up at our place from all over Copenhagen and are then fully refurbished by our skilled mechanics. We fix the parts that can be fixed, and replace the parts that needs replacement. We make sure every bike works properly.

You might want to join our customer club which will net you a 20% discount every reparation done in our workshop.

Do you know what you want? Then go straight our selection of recycled bikes. Otherwise you can get good advice in the Bike Guide

It is very important that you buy a bike
with a frame size that fits you!

Stand against a wall and using a book hold it in between your legs into your groin
and make sure it's flat against the wall.

Measure the height from the book to the floor. (as shown in the picture)

Use the table below to find your frame size


If you are in doubt do not hesitate to call us, or come down to the shop and we will find the bike for your needs.
