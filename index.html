OUR REINCARNATED BIKES
AND WORKSHOP





See our selection of reused bikes


All Buddha Bikes are reincernated second hand bikes. The old metal ends up at our place from all over Copenhagen and are then fully refurbished by skilled mechanics. We fix the parts that can be fixed, and replace the parts that needs replacement. We make sure every bike works properly.
